Skiing

Fallbrook is used as a cross country and downhill ski facility from late December to early March. Hiking trails are used as cross country ski trails in the winter and the large open hill is used for downhill skiing. The hill features a sustained pitch suitable for making a few downhill turns. From 1959 to 1979 Fallbrook operated a tow rope to bring skiers to the top of the hill. Since the closing of the tow rope, skiers hike to the top of the hill using the hiking trails.
